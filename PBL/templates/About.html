  About Us

Why EACare?

EA Care is a cloud based platform, which connects people facing a medical emergency with validated first responders via website or phone call.

The vision of EA Care is to make emergency response available to anyone, anytime, anywhere – with just one tap on a mobile phone, across India by 2024.

Currently the service is available in top 3 cities in Maharashtra which includes : Mumbai , Navi Mumbai and Thane.

The network aggregates existing emergency medical resources which include – Ambulances and other medically certified and trained volunteers who are validated and verified.
